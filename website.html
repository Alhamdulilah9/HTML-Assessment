The place menu

Appetizers

  • Stuffed Mushrooms

    Mushroom caps filled with a mixture of cheeses, garlic, breadcrumbs, and herbs.

  • Shrimps Cocktail

    Chilled cooked shrimp served with a tangy tomato-based cocktail sauce.

  • Samosas

    indian pastries filled with spiced potatoes, onions, peas, and lentils.

Main Courses

  • Pounded Yam

    Pounded yam with Egusi and Assorted meat.

  • Beans and Plantain

    A simple and nutritious meal, often consisting of black-eyed peas or honey beans cooked until tender and served with ripe fried plantains.

  • Ofada Rice and Ayamase Sauce

    Ofada rice is a locally produced variety of rice which is typically served with a spicy sauce called ayamase or ofada stew, made with green bell peppers, scotch bonnet peppers, onions, and assorted meats.

Desserts

  • Puff Puff

    Puff-puff is often rolled in sugar or served with a side of dipping sauce.

  • Boli

    Roasted plantains that are often served with groundnut (peanut) sauce or pepper sauce.

  • Masa or Waina

    Rice cakes served with sugar or honey.
